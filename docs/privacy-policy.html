1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

User-Generated Content:

  • Audio recordings you create for tours
  • Tour names, descriptions, and metadata
  • Comments and reviews on tours
  • Profile information and preferences

Payment Information (Professional Tier):

  • We do not directly store payment card information
  • Payment processing is handled securely by Google Play Billing and Stripe
  • We receive transaction confirmations and subscription status

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Location Data:

  • Precise GPS coordinates when you create or play tours (required for core functionality)
  • Location is collected in real-time during tour playback to trigger audio at waypoints
  • Location history is stored anonymously for analytics purposes
  • You can control location permissions through your device settings, but this may limit app functionality

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version (Android version)
  • Unique device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • App version
  • Language preference

Usage Data:

  • Tours you've played and created
  • Playback duration and completion rates
  • Waypoints reached
  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • Time and date of app usage

Audio Data:

  • We store audio files you record for tour waypoints
  • Audio is processed locally on your device and uploaded to our servers only when you choose to share tours publicly

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

Google Sign-In:

  • If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from Google

Third-Party Integrations:

  • Google Maps API data (map tiles, place information)
  • Wikipedia content (used for auto-generated tours)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Provide tour services: Enable GPS-triggered audio playback during tours
  • Tour creation: Save and manage your recorded audio tours
  • Account management: Authenticate users and maintain account security
  • Payment processing: Manage subscriptions and process payments for paid tours

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with tours to improve content quality
  • Bug fixes: Diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Feature development: Identify popular features and user needs

2.3 Communication

  • Service updates: Notify you about app updates, new features, or changes
  • Customer support: Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Marketing (opt-in): Send promotional content about new tours or features (you can opt out)

2.4 Advertising (Free Tier Only)

  • Display personalized ads based on your interests using Google AdMob
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Public Information

  • Tours you mark as "public" are visible to all Narratour users
  • Your display name and profile picture are visible on tours you create
  • Reviews and comments you post are public

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our Services:

Google Services:

  • Firebase Authentication: User account management
  • Firebase Cloud Storage: Audio file hosting
  • Google Maps API: Map display and location services
  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement serving (free tier users only)

Payment Processors:

  • Google Play Billing: In-app subscription management
  • Stripe: Tour monetization payments (Professional tier)

Infrastructure:

  • Cloud hosting providers: Server hosting and data storage
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): Fast audio file delivery

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to:

  • Protect our legal rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity
  • Protect the safety of our users or the public

3.4 Business Transfers

If Narratour is acquired or merged with another company, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice in the app before your information is transferred.

4. Data Retention

Account Data:

  • We retain your account information as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, we delete your personal information within 30 days

Tour Content:

  • Tours you create are retained indefinitely unless you delete them
  • If you delete a tour, it is permanently removed within 7 days

Analytics Data:

  • Anonymized usage data is retained for up to 2 years for analysis

Backup Data:

  • Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information through your account settings
  • Update your profile information, email, or display name
  • Download your data (export tours as JSON or GPX files)
  • Delete your account and all associated data

5.2 Location Data

  • Control location permissions through your device settings (Settings > Apps > Narratour > Permissions)
  • Note: Disabling location will prevent tour playback functionality

5.3 Marketing Communications

  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails via the link in any marketing email
  • You will still receive transactional emails (e.g., password resets, subscription confirmations)

5.4 Ad Preferences

  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
    • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • You will still see ads, but they won't be personalized

5.5 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)
  • Right to object to processing of your data
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at dannemrus@gmail.com

5.6 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know if we sell or disclose your information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (not applicable - we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Secure storage: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with secure token management
  • Access controls: Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

Organizational Safeguards:

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

No system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password and keep your account credentials confidential.

8. International Data Transfers

Data Protection Standards:

  • For EEA users, we comply with GDPR requirements through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
